Evelyn

Michelle Yeoh plays Evelyn Quan Wang, a dissattisfied and overwhelmed laundromat owner, and as several other versions of Evelyn in alternate universes.

Waymond

Ke Huy Quan plays Waymond Wang, Evelyn's meek and goofy husband whose humanist existentialism is the antidote to Jobu's nihilism; and as Alpha-Waymond, from the Alphaverse; and other versions of Waymond in alternate universes.

Joy

Stephanie Hsu plays Joy Wang, Evenlyn's daughter, as well as Jobu Tupaki, Alpha-Evelyn's omnicidal daughter whose growing nihilism is a threat to the entire multiverse.
