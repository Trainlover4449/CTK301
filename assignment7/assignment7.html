About The Movie

Everything Everywhere All At Once is a 2022 American independent film written and directed by Daniel Kwan and Daniel Scheinert, who produced it with Anthony and Joe Russo and Jonathan Wang; the film incorporates elements from a number of genres and film mediums, including absurdist fiction, comedy-drama, surreal comedy, science fiction, fantasy, martial arts films, immigrant narrative, and animation. Michelle Yeoh stars as Evelyn Quan Wang, a Chinese-American immigrant who, while audited by the IRS, discovers that she must connect with parallel universe versions of herself to prevent a powerful being from destroying the multiverse.

The movie utilizes an everything bagel as the symbol of the multiverse, and is not the first movie to use bagels as the symbol of the multiverse. 2023's Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse also utilized this principle, and there is even a reference to Everything Everywhere All at Once that is hidden in Across the Spiderverse.
